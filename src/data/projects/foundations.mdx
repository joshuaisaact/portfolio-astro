A 24-hour hackathon project. Team of four, I led the frontend. We built a dashboard that lets you search, filter, and compare gender pay gap data across 11,000+ UK companies. Won Best in Category against 20+ teams.

The data is public but buried in government CSVs that nobody reads. We built the interface that makes it browsable.
