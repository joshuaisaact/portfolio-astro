Pick two LLMs. Give them personalities. Make them argue. AIgument pits models like GPT-4o, Claude, and Gemini against each other in multi-round debates on any topic you like.

The fun is in the personality system. A Sassy Drag Queen debating a Noir Detective about whether water is wet, cranked to "Extra Hot" spiciness, produces results that are genuinely hard to stop reading. Debates are saved and shareable.
