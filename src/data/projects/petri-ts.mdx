A TypeScript Petri net engine published on npm. Supports defining nets with places and transitions, firing them, performing reachability and terminal state analysis, checking invariants, and exporting Graphviz DOT. Includes a load/fire/save dispatcher for persisting net instances in production (bring your own DB transaction).
